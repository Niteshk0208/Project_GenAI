What is Generative AI?

Generative AI is a type of artificial intelligence that has the ability to generate new and original outputs based on a set of inputs or data. This technology uses deep learning algorithms to analyze and understand patterns in data, and then creates new data based on those patterns. Generative AI is being used in a wide range of applications, from art and design to music and language generation.

Why it is important?

Generative AI is important because it has the potential to revolutionize the way we approach creativity, problem-solving, and decision-making. With generative AI, we can quickly and easily generate new ideas and solutions, explore different scenarios and possibilities, and automate complex tasks. This technology can help us be more productive, efficient, and innovative in everything we do.
